Intelligent Agents

This module looks at a very exciting, momentum-filled fields within computing: Intelligent Agents. Agents are intelligent and innovative software programs that can act according to their beliefs, to achieve their intended goals, while simultaneously understanding and navigating their environment. They can be deployed in a wide range of arenas across the private and public sector to achieve various socio-economic goals.
