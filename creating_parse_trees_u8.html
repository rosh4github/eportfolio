Creating Parse Trees

The goal here is to create a constituency-based parse tree for the below phrases. An online syntax tree generator was utilized to produce these images (Fingtam Languages, 2020).

The government raised interest rates.

The internet gives everyone a voice.
The man saw the dog with the telescope.
References:
mshang.ca. (n.d.). Syntax Tree Generator. [online] Available at: http://mshang.ca/syntree/.
Fingtam Languages. (2020) How to make a syntax tree (Hand drawn and digital). Available from: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CeuhQ3s-Jss&t=742s [Accessed 02 April 2024].
