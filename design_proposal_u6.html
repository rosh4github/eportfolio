Intelligent Digital Forensics MAS

Enron Corporation's legal team is working on resolving recent press rumours regarding illegal activity within the company. RMRK Consultants are tasked with helping the team in analysing their internal personnel communication, to identify any individuals that may be behind the alleged illegal activities. This report discusses the proposed design, testing, and suggested implementation of an agent-based model that aids the legal team in conducting internal digital forensics.
